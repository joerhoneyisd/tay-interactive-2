What this is

Feeling overwhelmed, anxious, or just not like yourself lately? You don't have to figure it out alone. Our counselors are trained to work with young people and won't judge you for anything you're going through.

You can talk about stress, family stuff, relationships, school, identity — whatever's on your mind. Everything you share stays private. Sessions are free, and you don't need insurance or your parents' permission to reach out.

What you can get

1-on-1 counseling
Regular sessions with a therapist who's yours.
Support groups
Connect with others who get what you're going through.
Crisis support
Someone to talk to 24/7 when things feel like too much.
Family sessions
Bring a parent or guardian if you want — totally optional.

How to get started

1
Reach out
Call, text, or fill out the online form. No pressure — you can just ask questions first.
2
Quick check-in call
A team member asks a few questions to match you with the right counselor. Takes about 15 minutes.
3
Book your first session
Pick a time that works — in person, video, or phone. That's it. You're in.

Good to know

Everything you share is confidential.
No insurance or ID required.
You can bring a friend to your first visit.
Wheelchair accessible & LGBTQ+ affirming.
