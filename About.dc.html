About Us

We're in your corner.

LA County Youth Services connects young people to the support they deserve — for free, without the runaround.

Our Mission

Every young person deserves a real shot.

Growing up isn't easy — and finding help shouldn't be the hard part. We built this so you can find real support for mental health, jobs, housing, school, and safety, all in one place.

No confusing forms. No judgment. Just people and programs ready to help you get where you're going.

Our Story

A county-wide effort, one young person at a time.

We started in 2019 as a small pilot connecting a handful of neighborhoods to youth resources. Today we partner with hundreds of community organizations across all of LA County.

Our team of counselors, case workers, and youth advocates work behind the scenes so that finding help feels simple — the way it should be.
